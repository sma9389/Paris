Spend the Day with Me in Paris

Starting the Journey with Matcha

While in Paris I had to try Matcha Social Club and I was shocked at how many matcha boxes there were!

Wandering the City

With our drinks in hand, we let the streets of Paris guide us and found ourselves behind the Louvre, taking in its beautiful architecture.

Unexpected Finds

After wandering the streets, we stumbled upon a bookstore in the middle of the streets and found a book filled with photos of Abu Dhabi!

By the Water

We continued our walk along the Seine, soaking in the view and the amazing weather.

Dinner Time

After a long day of exploring, we treated ourselves to an amazing meal at Bambini, the perfect pause before the night lights.

Sparkling Ending

After dinner, we headed to the Eiffel Tower since it was nearby and we got there just in time to see it glow with sparkles!
